About Us

The Adventure Awaits

Cvc Tours & Travel is a tours & travel company established in 2021. The company specializes in creating distinctive inbound and outgoing tours and travel experiences for both businesses and people, both locally and globally.

We are quite proud of this growth, which was made possible by our highly driven staff members, deep understanding of the business, and years of hands-on experience. We are aiming to quickly establish ourselves as a premier, reliable, and creative partner in Africa and beyond.

We offer individualized, socially conscious safaris and beach vacations.

We have made a commitment to providing you with a hearty, exceptional service that offers value for your money. We provide beach holidays, city excursions, hotel reservations, and airport transfers. We also provide wildlife photography safaris, bird watching safaris, mid-range safaris, and luxury safaris. You can take the World Adventures with the help of our helpful personnel, who are well-trained and knowledgeable.
